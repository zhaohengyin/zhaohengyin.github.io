Biography

I am an M.Phil. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. I also work closely with Prof. Yang Gao at Tsinghua University IIIS and Prof. Xiaolong Wang at UCSD.

I obtained my B.Sc. degree of Computer Science (Honored Class) and Mathematics advised by Prof. Wu-Jun Li at Nanjing University in 2021. During my undergraduate time, I also conducted research in Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka's Lab at UC Berkeley.


[September 2022: New] I am applying for a Ph.D. position on Robot Learning starting from Fall 2023. Shoot me an E-mail if you are interested.

Research

I am developing novel algorithms for robot learning. In particular, I am working on imitation learning and reinforcement learning. I have proposed imitation learning algorithms that can leverage cross domain expert data (ICRA'22), learn more sample efficiently with MCTS planning (NeurIPS'22), and generalize better with relational inductive bias (ICLR'23 under review). I also study the application of these algorithms on real robot platforms.
