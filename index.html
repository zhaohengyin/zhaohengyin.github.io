Biography

I am an M.Phil. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. I also work closely with Prof. Yang Gao at Tsinghua University IIIS and Prof. Xiaolong Wang at UCSD.

I obtained my B.Sc. degree of Computer Science advised by Prof. Wu-Jun Li at Nanjing University in 2021. During my undergraduate time, I spent some time in Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka's Lab at UC Berkeley.


[September 2022: New] I am applying for a Ph.D. position on Robot Learning starting from Fall 2023. Shoot me an E-mail if you are interested.

Research

I am developing novel algorithms for computational sensorimotor learning. In particular, I focus on imitation learning and reinforcement learning. I have proposed imitation learning algorithms that can leverage cross domain expert data (ICRA'22), learn more sample efficiently (NeurIPS'22), and generalize better with relational bias (ICLR'23 under review). I also study the application of these algorithms on real robot platforms.
